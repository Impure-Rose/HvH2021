What is HvH?

HVH is an abreviation for Humans versus Humans, 3 day game where players complete missions using nerf style blasters.

When does this take place?

HVH takes place the first week of SCAD's winter quarter.

How can I join?

Come to opening night, or come to a mission and find a mod.

Winter quarter is soooo far away, what can I do in the meantime?

SNUG (Savannah Nerf Underground):

takes place every Thursday from 8-10pm in the Hive Courtyard between A and B. We play both game modes from Humans versus Humans, and Humans versus Zombies. There you can meet other members and form friendships, and teams for HvH and HvZ.

HvZ (Humans versus Zombies):

This is a weeklong game of tag with nerf blasters. Taking place the first week of spring quarter, fight against the growing horde of zombies, or join them and overtake the human resistance!
